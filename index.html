PROJECTS

Read2Be

During last semester one of our university projects was LEI (Informatics Engineering Project). For this, me and my friends wanted to make a webapp that allowed us to save our 'plan-to-read' books, as well as the 'reading', 'dropped', and 'finished' ones. I was responsible for the frontend of this, using Vue.js and Vuetify.

Japanese Flashcards

This summer I made some spreadsheets with japanese words, along with their readings and meanings. I wanted to put those in flashcards, but regular flashcard apps only allow you to have two sides. However, I needed more, as sometimes I wanted to show only the reading, or only the meaning. This was a fun experiment with Vue.js made in 2 days.
